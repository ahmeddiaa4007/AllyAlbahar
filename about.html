Welcome to The Best Travel Company in Egypt!


We are thrilled to have you here and share with you what makes us stand out in the world of travel. Whether you are a seasoned traveler or embarking on your first adventure, we are here to make your dream vacation a reality.






At Ally AlBahar,


We believe that travel is not just about visiting new places, but about experiencing new cultures, creating unforgettable memories, and broadening your horizons. We are passionate about helping our clients discover the wonders of Egypt, a country rich in history, culture, and natural beauty.






With years of experience in the industry,


Our team of travel enthusiasts is dedicated to providing exceptional service and personalized attention to each and every client. We understand that planning a trip can be overwhelming, so we are here to take the stress out of the process and ensure a smooth and seamless journey.

From the majestic pyramids of Giza to the stunning beaches of the Red Sea, from the bustling streets of Cairo to the tranquil oases of the Western Desert, Egypt has something to offer for every type of traveler. Whether you are looking for a cultural immersion, an adventurous safari, a relaxing beach getaway, or a combination of it all, we have got you covered.






Our commitment to excellence,

Your safety, comfort, and satisfaction are our top priorities.
